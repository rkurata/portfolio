UX Design
Mobile App
About: Buddy System is a web-based mobile app was designed to meet students' needs by taking the scheduling hassle and intimidation factor out of talking to professors. Students can use Buddy System to form group appointments with their peers and professors, based on similar questions or topics that they would like to discuss.This creates a more relaxed conversational environment, allowing students to establish relationships with their professors, which can serve as valuable resources for students' future endeavours.

Tools: Needfinding interviews, storyboards, paper protyping, Nielsen's 10 usability heuristics, user testing, online testing, NodeJS, HTML, CSS
This project was ranked Top in Studio and was evaluated by Scott Klemmer and Don Norman
